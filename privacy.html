Privacy Policy & Data Protection

Last Updated: February 2024

At SecureSpark & CCTV Specialist, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal data. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information in compliance with STQC and data protection standards.

When you request a quotation or contact us, we collect:

  • Name and contact details (Phone, Email)

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used strictly for:

3. Data Security

We implement robust technical and organizational measures to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or modification of your personal data. Our website uses SSL encryption for all form submissions.

4. User Consent

By using our contact forms, you consent to the collection and processing of your data for the purpose of fulfilling your service requests.
